FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Here are answers to some of the most common questions I receive as a freelance designer. If you don't see your question here, feel free to reach out—I'm happy to help!

1.WHAT SERVICES DO YOU OFFER?
I offer UI/UX design, graphic design, web design, and branding services. Each project is tailored to meet your specific needs and business goals.
2.HOW DOES THE DESIGN PROCESS WORK?
My process includes discovery, research, wireframing, design, testing, and delivery. I keep you involved at every step to ensure the final product exceeds your expectations.
3.HOW LONG DOES A PROJECT USUALLY TAKE?
Project timelines vary depending on complexity. A simple website takes 2-3 weeks, while comprehensive branding projects can take 4-6 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.
4.WHAT DO I NEED TO PROVIDE BEFORE STARTING A PROJECT?
I'll need your project brief, brand guidelines (if any), content, images, and any specific requirements. Don't worry if you don't have everything—I'll help guide you through what's needed.
5.DO YOU OFFER REVISIONS?
Yes! I include up to 3 rounds of revisions in all my projects. Additional revisions can be accommodated for an extra fee. My goal is to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result.
6.HOW DO I GET STARTED?
Simply reach out through the contact form or email me directly. We'll schedule a consultation to discuss your project, timeline, and budget. From there, I'll create a customized proposal for your needs.
