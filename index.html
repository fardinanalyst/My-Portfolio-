Role: Product Analyst

Tools: SQL • Python (Pandas, SciPy, Statsmodels) • Power BI • A/B Testing

Overview

Spotify introduced a new Reward Ad format — users could watch one short ad to enjoy 30 minutes of ad-free listening. I designed and analyzed an A/B test to measure its effect on engagement and conversions among 200K users.

Key Highlights

Nykaa CSAT Decline Analysis

Tools: Python (Pandas, Seaborn) • Power BI • Excel

Problem

Nykaa’s Customer Satisfaction (CSAT) dropped 15% in Q1 2025 — management requested a root-cause analysis and a recovery strategy.

Methodology

  • Combined 116K+ rows from survey, ticket, and order datasets to create a unified analytics view.
  • Performed correlation analysis (CSAT vs Delay Days = −0.48), Pareto analysis, and customer segmentation.
  • Drilled into delivery, support, and order-accuracy funnels to identify top pain points.

Key Findings

  • Delivery delays strongly correlate with lower CSAT scores.
  • Support response inefficiencies contribute significantly to negative feedback.
  • Wrong-item orders are a recurring issue in specific fulfillment centers.

Recommendations

  • Optimize warehouse operations to reduce average delivery delay days.
  • Integrate AI chatbots and SLA-based routing to reduce support response times.
  • Introduce targeted QA checks at high-error fulfillment centers and a corrective training loop.
  • Run a short-term “express recovery” program (owned by ops + support) to proactively fix recent negative cases.

Business Outcome

Addressing the top three issues (delivery delays, support inefficiency, wrong-item orders) could recover approximately ~80% of lost CSAT within 2–3 months, based on impact simulations.

Skills Demonstrated

Root Cause Analysis • 5C Framework • Correlation Analysis • Customer Experience Analytics
